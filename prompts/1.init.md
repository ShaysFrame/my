### What a SKILL.md Is

The format is dead simple — it's a markdown file with YAML frontmatter that teaches an AI model *how to behave* for a specific task:

```markdown
---
name: my-skill-name
description: When Claude should activate this skill (trigger phrases, use cases)
allowed-tools: Read, Write, Bash, WebFetch   # optional
---

# Instructions

Everything below here is the instruction set the AI follows.
```

The `description` field is the most critical — it's what determines when the skill activates. The body is your prompt/workflow. That's the whole format. No code, no dependencies — just structured instructions.
